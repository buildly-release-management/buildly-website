Our Story

Our mission is to build inclusive communities where ideas are shared easily and teams work on innovative ideas rather than rebuilding the wheel. The founding of Buildly was based on several startups that most of the original founding team worked on, as well as the open source projects we built or contributed to over the last 10 years.

We are all passionate open source supporters and life long software developers and tech enthusiasts, and we have founded Buildly based on our previous experience and as an evolution of our desire to focus on user experience and craftsmanship in every phase of software development.
