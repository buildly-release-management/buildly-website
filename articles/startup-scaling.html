MVP: Build to Learn

At the very beginning, your product strategy should be ridiculously simple: learn as fast as possible. You're not building a company yet. You're testing a hypothesis. The goal isn't to scale â€” it's to prove that what you're building solves a real problem for real people.

Mindset Shift:

  • Manual work is fine. Incomplete UX is fine. Your roadmap is short because it should be.

What Matters Right Now:

  • Can people understand what the product does?
  • Do they want to use it again?
  • Can you learn something useful from early adopters?
