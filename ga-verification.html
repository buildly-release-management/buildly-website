Step 5: Common Issues

If GA isn't working, check these:
  • Ad Blockers: Disable ad blockers (uBlock Origin, AdBlock Plus, etc.)
  • Privacy Settings: Disable "Prevent Cross-site Tracking" in Safari
  • Browser Extensions: Try in incognito/private mode
  • Network: Corporate firewalls might block Google's servers
  • Time: New implementations take 24-48 hours to show in standard reports
